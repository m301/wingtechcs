Wingtech's mission is to enhance technical skill's of bbdian's

WINGTECH is the official forum of the department of Computer Science and Engineering and the School of Computer Application,BBD University.The forum derives its name from the community's core principle to promote innovation in all the spheres and provide a uniform platform to the students in order to showcase their technical and managerial skills.The forum was officially formulated in the year 2015 under the mentorship of Prof.(Dr.) Reena Srivastava,Dean of School of Computer Applications,BBDU,and the Head of Department of Computer Science and Engineering,BBDU,who also stands to be the faculty convener of Wingtech.The forum's General Secretary is Mr.Shubham Chaubey.

Wingtech intends to promote and enhance the knowledge and interest of students towards the dynamic world of computer science including programming languages,web development,mobile computing etc and provide a platform for the students to showcase their technical talents.
